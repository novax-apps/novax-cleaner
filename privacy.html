Privacy Policy for NOVAX Cleaner

Last updated: June 2026

In one sentence: We don't collect any data because the app is 100% offline and physically cannot connect to the internet.

Data Collection

NOVAX Cleaner does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app operates 100% offline and does not request the INTERNET permission in its AndroidManifest.xml. No analytics, no advertising identifiers, no usage data, no crash reports.

Permissions

The app requests only storage access permissions (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE) to analyze and clean files. These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality. We do not request internet, location, contacts, camera, microphone, phone state, or any other permissions.

Third Parties

We do not share data with third parties because we have no data to share. The app contains no third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, or crash reporting.

Verification

You can verify our privacy claims yourself:

  1. Open Android Settings > Apps > NOVAX Cleaner > Permissions
  2. Confirm: only storage permissions are listed
  3. No internet, location, contacts, or camera permissions

In-App Purchases

Premium purchases are processed through Google Play's billing system. We do not handle payment information directly and do not store purchase history on our servers.

Contact

For questions: visit our GitHub page or join the waitlist on our website.

This policy applies to NOVAX Cleaner version 1.0 and all future versions.
